Solimo Blossom Jumbo Creative Colouring Book for Kids, Level 1

> by Solimo (Author)

The house of Solimo brings to you this jumbo colouring book Level 1 for the early development of your kids. The book contains A3-size pages with vibrant images of animals, plants and cartoons among others, helping to make children familiar with the elements of nature and their surroundings. A reference guide is provided for the colours in the book. The bold outlines and large pictures help kids colour neatly within the borders. Each book contains 34 different topics suitable for children between 3 to 5 years of age. Let your children express their creativity with this Blossom colouring book from Solimo.
